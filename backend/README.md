# AxxessHackProject
our project for Axxess Hack...trsut we got it

We will record the patient and doctor conversation, we will then feed it into our AI model, and then we will have it analyze the key parts and give a summary for the patient with time stamps to indicate the important parts. Then based on the audio, we generate suggestions on what medicine to prescribe to the patient, but before we do that, we have a human doctor in the loop to verify the information is correct. If it is, we send it to the patient, and if not, the doctor can write a manual suggestion.
